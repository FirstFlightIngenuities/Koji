INSTRUCTIONS
Koji is a two part game

- Part combining colors


- Part uncovering hidden letters

INSTRUCTIONS
At the beginning of each game, you are shown the definition of a hidden word
That word is hidden in the large rectangle located in the lower half of the game board
Color Combining
1) Based on these top color combinations
2) Is it likely that combining these two colors will produce the result shown?
3) Press the green check for 'Yes' or the red X for 'No', you have 6 seconds to answer.
Letter Guessing
4) Every time you answer correctly, you'll see small pieces of the lower rectangle removed to reveal parts of the hidden word.
Note: The letters of the word are twisted and rotated.
5) After 3 correct color combination answers, you get a chance to guess a letter of the hidden word.
6) Guess all the letters correctly, and you win the game!

Koji

  • Decide if the result of a color combination is correct and uncover hidden letters. Figure out all the hidden letters and you win!

  • At the beginning of the game, you are given the definition of a word that is hidden in the large rectangle in the lower half of the game board. In order to reveal parts of that hidden word, you must first answer a color combining question: Based on the color combinations shown in the top half of the game board, does the result of the combination of the two colors in the bottom half of the game make sense? If you think it does, press the green "check" button. If not, press the red "X" button.

  • Every time you guess correctly, small segments of the large rectangle are removed to reveal parts of the hidden word. The letters of the word are twisted and rotated to make the game more challenging. After every 3rd correct color combining guess, you will get a chance to guess at one of the hidden letters. Correctly choose all the letters of the hidden word (correctly chosen letters will have green borders) and you win!

  • Xs:

    For every wrong color combination guess, you get an X. For every incorrect letter guess, you get an X. If time runs out before you answer a color combination question, you get an X. Four Xs and game is over.

  • Point values:

    Correctly guess which colors will combine to match: +5
    Correctly guess a hidden letter: varies from +5 to +50 depending on how much of the hidden letters have been revealed.
